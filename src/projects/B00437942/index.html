    Saket & Rajesh

    Saket Pandit Born in Mumbai and raised in six different cities throughout India, Saket was influenced early on by the culture and tradition of the plethora of varieties of Indian cuisine. For Saket, food is a very important part of his life. His father, Sudhendu, was the director of multiple hotel restaurants in India, and was responsible for curating new food concepts and managing restaurant openings. His mother, Neha taught Saket as a young boy how to cook with passion, from kneading naan breads to blending unique spices.
    On another hand, during his MBA program in Paris, Rajesh often found himself cooking simple and authentic Indian cuisine in his dorm room, as it reminded him of the bustling sound, colorful sight, and aromatic smell of the street food of India back home.
    With the opening of Curried, Saket and Rajest are aiming to replicate this traditional Indian street food and cuisine in Paris.
