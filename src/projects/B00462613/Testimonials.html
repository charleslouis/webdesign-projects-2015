Juan Carlos

Freeletics is special in what they have to offer as a way of social engagement with other “Free athletes” (their term, not mine) and the motivation they build in you by transforming exercise into a sport (which you can do alone or in a group). Recently I was approached by a stranger who was also doing freeletics and he suggested we workout together. Once you start Freeletics, you will recognise other free athletes by the frantic pace of the exercises. Soon after you visit their website you will be hooked by the testimonials and progress videos posted by the different users.

Cordovez

Done! 15 weeks of the Freeletics Strength Workout completed and I feel so much better for it! My strength & stamina have increased massively, I have dropped two waist sizes in jeans (now a 30 inch and even they are a bit big!).Body fat % has dropped by 6% from 19.9% to 13.9%. I’m not lying when I say that physically, I am in the best shape of my life and so pleased I have stuck at this.I would definitely recommend Freeletics to anyone who is looking to get into shape and lacks tone & definition (as I did when I started) -I did the Strength coach and it contained the right amount of cardio to strength training for me to achieve my goal of building muscle mass whilst losing fat. I do think my results would have been more impressive if I was eating a calorie surplus (needed to build muscle) but I was operating on a deficit as my main goal was to lose my beer belly.
