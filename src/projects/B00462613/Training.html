ENDURANCE, STANDARD AND STRENGTH – WHAT DO THEY MEANl

There are three different difficulty grades for each workout and exercise.Endurance workouts and exercises consist of less complex movements which allow you to train faster with a higher number of reps. They primarily promote cardiovascular endurance: they literally take your breath away. Don’t underestimate them! They are also suitable for introducing beginners to the sport of Freeletics.

WHAT IS MEANT BY THE “AFTERBURN EFFECT”?

The greater the effort, the greater the increase in metabolism compared to a resting state. And the greater this difference, the longer the body needs to return to normal. During this period, your organism consumes a large amount of energy in the form of calories. This is the so-called afterburn effect.

HIT

High intensity training means short-term training under maximum stress. A variety of systems, such as the circulatory system, heart, lungs, muscles, nerves, and your metabolism are challenged and stimulated in a most intense way
