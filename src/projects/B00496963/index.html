About us

Chare is a sharing platform for homeworks. It is not like every one of us has never struggled with a homework at school. Sometimes (well... all the times), a little help and let's say it clearly... a little of cheating, is highly appreciated. On this website you will find any homeworks that nice fellows are willing to share with you. With a bit of luck, you will find here the exact same homework from last year that the one you are struggling with now. Because let's face it, not all the professors bother with changing subjects year to year. In any case, this is also a great tool to check on reports from qualified students (and if not, don't worry, that is what the rates are made for), about a lot of different fields.So, relax, enjoy and chare!
