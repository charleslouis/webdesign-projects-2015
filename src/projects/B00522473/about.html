About this website

This website’s objective is to promote my comics in an ergonomic and aesthetic way. I have been reading web comics for a while now, and what has always shocked me was the lack of ergonomic while browsing those websites. It has always struck me, as comic authors are master when it comes to composing a panel, but never apply these principles to their websites. Most of the time, this results in a very confusing, overcharged website, with many functionalities, all dispatched all over the pages. Hence I decided to create my own version of a web comic website. I really wanted this project to be sober, while effectively supporting comics treating about topics and genres I feel concerned with, such as introspective and abstracted questioning, oneiric fiction and pragmatic fantasy. I really hope you will enjoy my work, as much comic-wise as design-wise, have fun reading, and of course, do not hesitate to comment!!

This is where I live !!
