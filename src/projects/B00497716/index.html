WELCOME TO MY VERY OWN WORLD TOUR!

First, thanks for stopping by! I’m Deborah Bompane, and this site will be my home as I plan on travelling around the world pretty soon.

My main goal is to share my journey, as well as share the hows and whys for others feeling adrift and looking for a change in their lives.

Indeed, My Very Own World Tour is not only a pure travelogue as it also represents a resource point to motivate and encourage other travelers through stories, advice, and photography. Many people dream of taking a similar trip and may find the courage and resources to make international travel a reality.

Feel free to check my travel posts through the Destinations section or the Themes one if you are looking for a specific theme-related information. You may also find useful travel tips under the Travel Tips section.

Wishing you a very pleasant visit and a sunny day!

-To make sure you will not miss a thing, do not forget to subscribe to updates via email-

-Déborah
