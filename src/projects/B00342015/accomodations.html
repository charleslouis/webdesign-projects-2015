Our advice is to spend a few days in Paris prior the wedding, then to stay in St Germain en Laye on the night before the ceremony. After the wedding on July 2nd, you can stay at the Villiers-le-Mahieu Castle or any other hotel in the surrounding! For your convenience, here is a list of hotels you can book at! Hurry in to book you room!

St Germain

en Laye

Set in Saint-Germain Castle’s park, the Pavillon Henri IV offers 4-star accommodations with amazing views of Paris and the Seine Valley.

Steps away from Saint-Germain Castle’s park, the Ermitage des Loges offers comfortable rooms and is close to the train station, giving easy access to Paris.

Paris

Outstanding value for money, the Hotel Vic Eiffel with chic orange and oyster-grey rooms, is a short walk from the Eiffel Tower, with the metro on the doorstep.

Renting out a typical Parisian's home through Airbnb is a fun and cheap alternative to the traditional hotel. It's definitly what we would go for!

Villiers

le Mahieu

After the wedding, stay with us at the beautiful Château de Villiers le Mahieu and join us for brunch on the next day!

Only 5km away from the Castle, the Auberge de l'Étoile offers great value for money. Rooms are comfortable and the service is warm and authentic.
