The Big Day

Saturday July 2nd, 2016



The Ceremony

Saint Germain en Laye's Church - July 2nd, 2016 at 3:00pm

The wedding ceremony will begin at 3:00pm in Saint Germain en Laye's Church, where me and my family used to go every Sunday. Located opposite Saint Germain's Castle and only 1 min walk from the RER station, the address is 4 Place Charles de Gaulle 78100 Saint Germain en Laye. In addition to the beauty of the Chruch, we are so happy to have my oncle Monsignor Maurice Piat to marry us. Oh and for those worrying to be lost in translation, rest assured that the ceremony will be in English. Yup, my oncle Mo is cool like that!

More info


The Reception

Château de Villiers le Mahieu - July 2nd, 2016 at 6:00pm

Even before we got the venue booked, Tyler told everybody we will marry in a castle in France so good thing it's actually happening! The cocktail will start at 6:00pm followed by dinner, a kickin' party and many surprises! As the Castle has been converted into a hotel, you' re most welcome to spend the night and join us for brunch in the morning!

More info



Directions

From the Church to Château de Villiers le Mahieu

For your convenience, we organized a bus to take you from the Church to the Castle. For those interested, the meeting point will be in front of Saint Germain en Laye's Church at 5:00pm. As the ceremony will finish at 4:00pm, you will have time to chill at one of the cafés by the Church. For those driving or taking a cab, count 40 min to get to the Castle. The adress 1 rue du Centre, 78770 Villiers Le Mahieu.

Accomodations

Our advice is to spend a few days in Paris prior the wedding, then to stay in St Germain en Laye on the night before the ceremony. After the wedding on July 2nd, you can stay at the Villiers-le-Mahieu Castle or any other hotel in the surrounding! For your convenience, here is a list of hotels you can book at! Hurry in to book you room!

St Germain

en Laye

Set in Saint-Germain Castle’s park, the Pavillon Henri IV offers 4-star accommodations with amazing views of Paris and the Seine Valley.

Steps away from Saint-Germain Castle’s park, the Ermitage des Loges offers comfortable rooms and is close to the train station, giving easy access to Paris.

Paris, the

City of Love

Outstanding value for money, the Hotel Vic Eiffel with chic orange and oyster-grey rooms, is a short walk from the Eiffel Tower, with the metro on the doorstep.

Renting out a typical Parisian's home through Airbnb is a fun and cheap alternative to the traditional hotel. It's definitly what we would go for!

Villiers

le Mahieu

After the wedding, stay with us at the beautiful Château de Villiers le Mahieu and join us for brunch on the next day!

Only 5km away from the Castle, the Auberge de l'Étoile offers great value for money. Rooms are comfortable and the service is warm and authentic.
