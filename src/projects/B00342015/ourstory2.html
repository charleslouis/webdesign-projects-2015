The Proposal - December 18th, 2014

From Juliette

"Tyler and I met on this boat, on the windy afternoon of May 28th, 2013. We were both solo travelers, heading back to Bali to catch our flight home on the next day. Tyler sat next me and we started chating. I remember I first asked "What happened to your eye?" Yup, he had a pretty nasty red eye after a week of current diving and he likes to say that's how he got me. Little we knew this would be the start of a beautiful story."



"24 hours in paradise and a bunch of skypes were enough for us to know we wanted to be together. Two month after we first met, I booked my ticket and flew to Vancouver. Some people thought we were insane, but now we got a pretty cool story to tell our future kids. From Vancouver to Lake Louise and Okanagan Wineyards, Tyler took me to most beautiful places but only him captured my heart forever."





"After we fell in love in Vancouver, we went through four month of long distance until Tyler came to Paris. It was so great to introduce him to my family and friends and to take him around Paris.It was truly one of the best Christmas."
