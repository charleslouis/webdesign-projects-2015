Webdesign project 2015

Note

*to others, on tech forums, to me on the class forum

Grading

  • Web/App project : 70%
  • Project report : 30%

Presentation

You’ll be presenting your project on monday 18th of July in front of your classmates as well as industry professionnals. Each student will have 5 minutes to present her/his project, including Q&A. Presentation and justification will contribute to the grading process. Consider this a learning opportunity as the purpose is mainly to let you expose your work and encourage you to gather feedback and justify your choices. You might hear two kind of questions : WHY ? (mostly) and HOW ?

Delivery

A zipped folder should be sent to the course tutor before 11am on monday 15th of june (delay will be penalized). This archive will encompass your project repository and your project report. The project archive should be name webdesign-2015-B00XXXXX. Mail object should be “Webdesign project 2015”. Penalty applied for bad naming conventions.

Indicative road map (waterfall)

Conception

  • What do you want to build ?
  • What problem are you trying to solve ?
  • Who is your audience ?
  • ...

Initiation

  • What are your goal and objectives ?
  • What are your success criterias (some are listed down below) ?
  • ...

Analysis

  • Who is your audience ?
  • What does your user like ? Look for ? Is concerned with ?
  • What are the context in which your site/app will be used ?
  • Run objective and subjective researches about your users
  • ...

Design

  • List your pages and define your site architecture
  • Build your content strategy mobile-first
  • Define your tone and design for emotions
  • Start your copywriting work
  • Gather non-textual content elements (photos, videos)
  • Wireframe mobile-first
  • Wireframe for larger screens if you want/need
  • Define your style : colors, typography/font, mood, atmosphere, icons
  • Create a style tile ?
  • You should now be able to complete your website-persona
  • ...

Construction

  • Pick up a front-end framework if you whish
  • Install WAMP/MAMP/LAMP if it is not present on your computer
  • Create a repository to welcome your project in your local server
  • Code your html based on your former researches
  • Style with CSS based on your former researches
  • ...

Testing

  • Test by yourself
  • Watch some others using it
  • Ask them to accomplish specifi tasks
  • Make the site/app available to others
  • Gather feedback, refine and refactor
  • ...

Production/implementation

  • Install filezilla
  • Get your server FTP credentials
  • What can you on your domain before going live ?
  • Go live
  • ...

Maintenance

  • Will you ?
  • Your report should show your maintenance concerns
  • What maintenance cost could you foresee ?
  • ...
